📐 Formula Sheet

Quick reference for all important physics equations

Kinematics

v = u + at
s = ut + ½at²
v² = u² + 2as
a = Δv/Δt

Newton's Laws

F = ma
F₁ = -F₂
F_friction = μN
W = Fs cos θ

Energy

KE = ½mv²
PE = mgh
E = mc²
P = W/t

Momentum & Collisions

p = mv
J = FΔt = Δp
m₁v₁ + m₂v₂ = (m₁+m₂)v_f
e = (v₂ - v₁)/(u₁ - u₂)

Waves & Oscillations

v = fλ
T = 1/f
y = A sin(ωt + φ)
I = P/A

Circular Motion

a_c = v²/r = ω²r
F_c = mv²/r
ω = 2πf = 2π/T
τ = Iα
